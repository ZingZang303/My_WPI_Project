My Town

Leesburg, Virginia is a charming town nestled in Loudoun County, just a stone's throw from the Potomac River. It offers a delightful small-town vibe with a modern twist.
The historic downtown area is a pedestrian's paradise, lined with brick sidewalks and bursting with trendy boutiques, art galleries, and inviting cafes. Foodies will love the diverse restaurant scene, with everything from farm-to-table fare to casual pub fare and trendy gastropubs.
Beyond the shops and restaurants, Leesburg offers a vibrant arts scene. Take a stroll and admire the public murals and sculptures scattered throughout town, or catch a live performance at the Tally Ho Theater. Outdoor enthusiasts can explore the nearby trails or parks, while shoppers can score deals at the Leesburg Premium Outlets. Leesburg offers a charming small-town escape with something for everyone.
