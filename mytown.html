My Town

Leesburg, Virginia is a charming town located about 33 miles west-northwest of Washington, D.C. Steeped in history, Leesburg was established in 1758 and is the county seat of Loudoun County. The town offers a delightful mix of old and new, with a vibrant historic district alongside modern amenities.
